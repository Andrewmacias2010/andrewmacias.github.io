About Me

Andrew Macias is an American public figure who became an instant internet icon in 2015. At just four years old, his heartwarming and highly relatable "Are you going to miss your mom?" back-to-school interview with KTLA news reporter Courtney Friel went viral globally, capturing the hearts of millions.

Over the years, Andrew has remained a beloved part of pop culture history. In 2023, he reunited with KTLA to celebrate the legacy of the clip and collaborated with Netflix on a prominent promotional campaign for the film Spy Kids: Armageddon. Today, Andrew continues to engage with his digital legacy while pursuing new creative opportunities.
